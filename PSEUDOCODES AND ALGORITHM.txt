ALGORITHM

5 Verify the l
6 Print "enter the width of the rectangle (w)"
7 Verify the w
8 Calculate area using the formula l*w

10 Print "Area of the rectangle"
11 Print "Perimeter of the rectangle"
12 STOP

1 START
